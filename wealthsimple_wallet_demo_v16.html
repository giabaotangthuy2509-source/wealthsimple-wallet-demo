One Wallet.

Wealthsimple Wallet is a mobile-first digital wallet that brings together chequing, savings, credit, investing, crypto, and tax information in one secure experience. It is built for a cardless world, designed for the digital money era, and positioned as a future-banking product inside the Wealthsimple ecosystem.

What the product does

All accounts in one place

Users can access Wealthsimple chequing, savings, credit, investment, crypto, and tax information from one wallet instead of switching between separate tools.

Digital cards for every account

Each account can appear as its own digital card, similar to a mobile wallet, so customers can choose the funding source they want for each purchase.

Crypto & investment payment

Customers can choose supported crypto or investment-backed payment cards and let Wealthsimple convert the amount needed for an approved purchase.

Business + personal support

Business users get business cards, reconciliation support, and tax visibility, while personal users also receive tax information such as capital gains tracking.

What it gives customers

Faster access: one secure place for money, cards, and account information.
Better convenience: fewer apps, fewer cards, fewer steps to pay.
Smarter visibility: integrated balances, activity, and tax-related information.
Stronger ecosystem: encourages everyday use of multiple Wealthsimple services.
Modern security: Face ID or password needed for private information or payment activation.
Flexible use: useful for students, professionals, investors, and small-business owners.

Tax Transparency Features

Capital gains preview: shows estimated gain/loss before using crypto or investments for payment.

Transaction log: records date, asset sold, market price, CAD value, and purpose.

Business card owners: receive reconciliation data for expense tracking and tax filing.

Prototype safeguard: crypto/investment payments should require consent, risk disclosure, and tax-impact visibility before activation.
